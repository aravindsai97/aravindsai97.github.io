Aravind Sai Sarathy

PhD in Curriculum & Instruction

I’m a 3rd year Ph.D. student in the Department of Curriculum & Instruction at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. My research sits at the intersection of learning sciences, metacognition, and educational technologies. Drawing from constructionist pedagogies, instructional design, and data science, I design pedagogical approaches and learning technologies that foster meaningful, data-informed learning experiences in both formal and informal settings. I’m always glad to talk with collaborators, educators, and ed-tech teams about turning learning-science insight into experiences and tools that people actually use.
