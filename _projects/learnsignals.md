**The problem.** By the time a low grade appears, it's often too late to intervene. LearnSignals turns clickstream and writing-process data into early, actionable signals teachers can act on the same week.

**My role.** Designed the prediction pipeline and the dashboard UX with input from a teacher advisory group.

**Outcome.** Deployed across **6 courses**; the early-warning flag identified at-risk students **~3 weeks earlier** than midterm grades, with teachers rating the alerts as "useful" or "very useful" in 80% of cases.
