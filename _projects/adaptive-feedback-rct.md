**The question.** Does *when* and *how* automated feedback is delivered change what learners actually retain?

**Approach.** A pre-registered, randomized field experiment across **4,000 learners** in an online course, comparing immediate vs. delayed and process- vs. outcome-framed feedback.

**Finding.** Process-framed, slightly delayed feedback produced the strongest retention — a result with direct implications for how ed-tech products design their feedback loops.
