**The problem.** AI-tutoring research is hard to compare because everyone evaluates on different tasks. OpenItemBank is a curated, openly licensed set of items and rubrics for consistent benchmarking.

**My role.** Curated and released the dataset; wrote the documentation and baseline evaluation scripts.

**Outcome.** Openly released with **2,000+ items**; adopted by several research groups for evaluating LLM tutors.
