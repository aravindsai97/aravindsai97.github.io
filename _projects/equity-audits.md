**The question.** Ed-tech is usually evaluated on average outcomes — which can hide who it fails. How do we surface those gaps before a tool ships?

**Approach.** Developed a reusable "equity audit" protocol combining disaggregated outcome analysis with qualitative interviews, then applied it to three widely used learning tools.

**Finding.** Two of the three tools that looked effective on average produced **no benefit — or harm — for specific learner groups**, with concrete, fixable design causes.
