2026

  1. LAK
    When Feedback Helps: Timing and Framing of Automated Feedback at Scale
    Aravind Sai, Jane Doe, and John Smith
    Proceedings of the International Conference on Learning Analytics and Knowledge (LAK), 2026
    Abs Bib HTML

    A pre-registered randomized field experiment with 4,000 learners testing how the timing and framing of automated feedback affect retention. Process-framed, slightly delayed feedback produced the strongest delayed-test performance.

2025

  1. L@S
    Designing an LLM Tutor for Productive Struggle
    Aravind Sai and Min Lee
    In Proceedings of the ACM Conference on Learning @ Scale (L@S), 2025
    Abs Bib HTML

    We present TutorLens, an LLM tutor designed to scaffold rather than solve, and a classroom evaluation showing improved conceptual post-test performance over an answer-giving baseline.

  2. JLS
    Equity Audits for Educational Technology: A Method and Three Case Studies
    Aravind Sai and Maria Garcia
    Journal of the Learning Sciences, 2025
    Abs Bib

    A reusable protocol for auditing whether ed-tech tools benefit all learner groups, applied to three widely used tools. Two tools effective on average showed no benefit—or harm—for specific subgroups.

  1. EDM
    Early Signals: Predicting Support Needs from Learning Process Data
    Aravind Sai and Rohan Patel
    In Proceedings of the International Conference on Educational Data Mining (EDM), 2024
    Abs Bib
